Paper Submission

Authors are requested to submit their Research Paper in MS Word in IEEE format at https://easychair.org/cfp/RTDEEE-2018 . Paper should have an Abstract of 200 words, 4-6 Key Words, Figures, Diagrams, Tables, Graphs and Reference in prescribed Format and Full Length of Paper not more than 6 Pages. Papers Selected in RTDEEE-2018 will be considered for Publication in UGC approved [listed as journal No. 48664 at UGC website] ISST Journal of Electrical & Electronics Engineering [ISSN: 0976-7363].
Three Best Papers Award shall be honoured by ISST by presenting a Memento and Certificate.
